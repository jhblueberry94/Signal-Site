Questions, answered

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your account at any time. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.

What's the difference between the Market, Personal, and Product pillars?

Market prompts help you comment on your industry. Personal prompts surface your story and lessons. Product prompts help you talk about what you build or sell , without sounding salesy.

Do you offer a free trial of Pro?

Not at the moment. Start on Free, see if Signal fits your routine, then upgrade when you're ready.

How does billing work for Team?

Custom invoicing, billed annually. Get in touch and we'll put together a quote based on seat count.
