How Signal’s pricing compares to Taplio, Supergrow and AuthoredUp

Most LinkedIn content tools use tiered pricing, where ideation features, analytics or scheduling sit behind higher plans. Taplio’s AI features start at around $65/month, Supergrow runs $19 to $39/month depending on tier, and AuthoredUp’s paid plans add analytics from around $12/month on top of its editor. Signal is one flat plan at £35/month with everything included: daily research-backed questions, Story Arc drafting, Tone of Voice calibration and LinkedIn scheduling.

Common questions about Signal's pricing

How much does Signal cost?

Signal is £35/month, or the equivalent in USD and EUR. One plan, everything included, with a 7-day free trial and no credit card required to start.

Is Signal's LinkedIn content tool free right now?

Yes. During our open testing period all users get full Pro access at no cost, for a limited time. Once the period ends, new users start a standard 7-day free trial before moving to £35/month.

What happens to existing users when the free period ends?

You'll receive an email before the period ends inviting you to start a paid plan. There are no automatic charges. You choose when to subscribe.

Do I need a credit card to start the free trial?

No. Start your 7-day trial with just an email address. You'll only be asked for payment details if you choose to continue after the trial.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your account settings at any time. You keep access until the end of your current billing period.

What are the three content pillars?

Market & Industry prompts help you comment credibly on your sector. Personal Journey prompts surface your story, lessons, and experience. Product & Service prompts help you talk about what you build or sell, without sounding like an ad.

How is Signal different from Taplio or Supergrow?

Signal is a thinking tool, not a content factory. Instead of generating posts from thin air, it asks you a focused question each morning and turns your own answer into a structured draft. The result sounds like you, because it is you.

How does the Team plan work?

Team plans are billed annually with a minimum of 5 seats. Email josh@blueberry-media.co.uk and we'll send a quote within 24 hours. Onboarding is handled by a team admin and includes a setup call.
